About Us

Jiangsu RuoYao Medical Equipment Co., Ltd was founded on January 24th 2018. It is located in China Medical City (CMC) in Taizhou City, China. It has a total area of 1500 square meters and total investments of 20 million Yuan. CMC is China's first national medical high-tech zone jointly built by the Ministry of Science and Technology, National Health and Family Planning Commission, China Food and Drug Administration, State Administration of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Jiangsu Provincial People's Government.

RuoYao’s product portfolio includes research, production and sales of type I, II and III medical equipment; the sales of medical plastic and rubber products, the research and development, transfer, consulting and promotion of medical technology; business management consulting, marketing research; conference and exhibition services; self-operated and act as an agent for import and export of all kinds of goods and technology (excluding goods or technology which import or export is banned or restricted by the nation); computer network system engineering services; the sales of machinery, electronics, stationary, craft and commodities; the production of sanitary and disposable medical supplies; the production and sales of masks.
